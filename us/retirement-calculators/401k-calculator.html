Frequently Asked Questions

How much should I contribute to my 401k?

At minimum, contribute enough to get your full employer match. Ideally, work toward 15% of your income.

What is employer matching?

Employers may match your contributions up to a percentage of your salary, typically 3-6%.

What are 401k contribution limits?

For 2024, the employee contribution limit is $23,000, or $30,500 if age 50 or older.
