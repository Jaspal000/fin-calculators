Frequently Asked Questions

How much do I need to retire?

Most experts suggest 25x your annual expenses or 80% of pre-retirement income.

When should I start saving for retirement?

The earlier the better. Starting in your 20s allows compound interest to work dramatically.

What is the 4% rule?

The 4% rule suggests you can safely withdraw 4% of your nest egg annually in retirement.
