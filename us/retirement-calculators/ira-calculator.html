Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Traditional and Roth IRA?

Traditional offers tax-deductible contributions and taxable withdrawals. Roth uses after-tax money but withdrawals are tax-free.

What are IRA contribution limits?

For 2024, the limit is $7,000, or $8,000 if age 50 or older.

Can I have both types of IRA?

Yes, but your total contributions cannot exceed the annual limit.
