Frequently Asked Questions

How much retirement income will I need?

Most retirees need 70-80% of pre-retirement income.

When should I take Social Security?

You can start at 62, but waiting until full retirement age or 70 maximizes your benefit.

What is a safe withdrawal rate?

The 4% rule is commonly used.
