Frequently Asked Questions

How are Social Security benefits calculated?

Benefits are based on your 35 highest-earning years, adjusted for inflation.

What is full retirement age?

Full retirement age is 66-67 depending on your birth year.

Can I work and collect Social Security?

Yes, but if under full retirement age, earnings above a limit may reduce your benefits.
