Frequently Asked Questions

What is CAGR?

Compound Annual Growth Rate measures the mean annual growth rate over a specified time period.

How do I calculate total return?

Total return includes capital gains plus dividends, divided by initial investment.

What is a good annual return?

Historically, the stock market averages 7-10% annually after inflation.
