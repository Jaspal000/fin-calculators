About Refinance Calculator

Wondering if refinancing is right for you? Our refinance calculator helps you determine your break-even point and potential savings. Compare your current loan terms with new offers to see how much you could save.

Use this calculator to make informed financial decisions. Adjust the inputs to see how different scenarios affect your results. All calculations are performed locally in your browser for privacy and security.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I refinance my mortgage?

Consider refinancing when rates drop at least 0.5-1% below your current rate.

What are typical closing costs?

Closing costs typically range from 2% to 6% of the loan amount.

How long does it take to break even?

Break-even periods typically range from 2-5 years.
