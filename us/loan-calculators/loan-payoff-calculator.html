About Loan Payoff Calculator

Use our loan payoff calculator to see how making extra payments can save you thousands in interest and help you become debt-free sooner. This powerful tool shows you exactly when you'll pay off your loan and how much interest you'll save by increasing your monthly payment.

Use this calculator to make informed financial decisions. Adjust the inputs to see how different scenarios affect your results. All calculations are performed locally in your browser for privacy and security.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much will extra payments save me?

Even small extra payments can save significant interest over time. Use this calculator to see your specific savings.

Should I pay off my loan early?

Consider your interest rate, other debts, and investment opportunities. High-interest loans are usually best to pay off first.

Can I make lump sum payments?

Most loans allow lump sum payments toward principal. Check with your lender for prepayment penalties.
