About Home Equity Calculator

Discover how much equity you've built in your home with our home equity calculator. This tool estimates your current home value minus your mortgage balance to show available equity.

Use this calculator to make informed financial decisions. Adjust the inputs to see how different scenarios affect your results. All calculations are performed locally in your browser for privacy and security.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is home equity calculated?

Home equity is your home's current market value minus your outstanding mortgage balance.

How much equity can I borrow?

Most lenders allow you to borrow up to 80-85% of your home's value minus your mortgage balance.

What can I use home equity for?

Common uses include home improvements, debt consolidation, and education expenses.
