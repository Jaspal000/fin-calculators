About Auto Loan Calculator

Planning to buy a new or used car? Our auto loan calculator helps you determine your monthly car payments and total loan cost. Factor in your down payment, trade-in value, sales tax, and interest rate to get an accurate estimate.

Use this calculator to make informed financial decisions. Adjust the inputs to see how different scenarios affect your results. All calculations are performed locally in your browser for privacy and security.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good interest rate for a car loan?

Good auto loan rates typically range from 3% to 6% for new cars and 4% to 8% for used cars.

How much should I put down on a car?

Aim for at least 20% down on a new car or 10% on a used car to avoid being upside down.

What loan term should I choose?

Shorter terms have higher payments but less total interest. Avoid terms longer than 60 months.
