Frequently Asked Questions

What is net worth?

Net worth is the difference between what you own (assets) and what you owe (liabilities).

What is a good net worth?

Net worth varies by age. A common benchmark is having your annual salary saved by age 30.

How often should I calculate net worth?

Monthly or quarterly tracking helps you see trends.
