Frequently Asked Questions

How is credit card interest calculated?

Most cards use daily periodic rate multiplied by average daily balance.

Should I pay more than the minimum?

Absolutely. Paying only minimums can take decades and cost thousands.

Should I transfer to a 0% APR card?

Balance transfers can save money if you pay off before the promotional rate expires.
