Frequently Asked Questions

What is the 50/30/20 rule?

Allocate 50% to needs, 30% to wants, and 20% to savings and debt repayment.

How do I track my expenses?

Use budgeting apps, spreadsheets, or review bank statements.

What if my expenses exceed income?

Look for areas to cut discretionary spending or increase income.
