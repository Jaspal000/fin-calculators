Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between short and long-term gains?

Short-term gains (held under 1 year) are taxed as ordinary income. Long-term gains have preferential rates.

How can I minimize capital gains tax?

Hold investments over a year and use tax-loss harvesting.

Do I pay tax on unrealized gains?

No, you only pay capital gains tax when you sell an asset.
