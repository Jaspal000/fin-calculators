About Income Tax Calculator

Calculate your federal income tax liability using current IRS tax brackets and rates. Our income tax calculator shows you exactly how much tax you owe based on your taxable income.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the current federal tax brackets?

Federal tax brackets range from 10% to 37% depending on your income and filing status.

How do tax brackets work?

The US uses a progressive tax system. Only income within each bracket is taxed at that rate.

What is taxable income?

Taxable income is your gross income minus deductions, exemptions, and adjustments.
