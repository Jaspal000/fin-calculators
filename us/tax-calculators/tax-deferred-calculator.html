Frequently Asked Questions

What does tax-deferred mean?

Tax-deferred means you don't pay taxes on contributions or growth until withdrawal.

How much can tax deferral save me?

Tax deferral can significantly increase your ending balance due to compounding.

Should I choose Roth or Traditional?

Traditional may be better if you expect lower taxes in retirement.
