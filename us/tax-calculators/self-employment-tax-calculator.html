About Self-Employment Tax Calculator

Freelancers, contractors, and small business owners can calculate their self-employment tax with ease. This tool computes both the employer and employee portions of Social Security and Medicare taxes.

Use this calculator to make informed financial decisions. Adjust the inputs to see how different scenarios affect your results. All calculations are performed locally in your browser for privacy and security.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the self-employment tax rate?

The self-employment tax rate is 15.3%, consisting of 12.4% for Social Security and 2.9% for Medicare.

Do I need to pay quarterly taxes?

If you expect to owe $1,000 or more in taxes, you should make quarterly estimated payments.

Can I deduct self-employment tax?

You can deduct the employer portion (50%) of self-employment tax.
